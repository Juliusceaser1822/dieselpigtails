Why Choose Diesel Pigtails?

OEM Quality

We supply genuine OEM‑grade connectors and pigtails. Using the correct terminals and seals ensures a perfect fit and a secure, long‑lasting connection. Avoid the misshapen terminals found in imported connectors and trust parts built to the same standards as your original harness.

Built for Durability

Diesel engines run hot and vibrate. Over time wiring harness connectors can crack or become brittle. Our pigtails are designed to cope with high‑heat locations around the turbo and engine bay so you can avoid intermittent codes and restore proper boost and fuel control.

Comprehensive Coverage

Whether you own a Cummins, Powerstroke, Duramax or Paccar‑powered truck, we offer repair harnesses and sensor pigtails to cover common failure points. From turbo VGT and fuel control actuators to injector and sensor connectors – we have you covered.

Same‑Day Shipping

When your truck is down, every hour counts. We keep thousands of connectors, terminals, seals, clips and repair tools in stock for immediate shipment. Get the parts you need, fast, and get back on the road.
